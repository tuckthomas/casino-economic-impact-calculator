# SaveFW Project Overview

**Save Fort Wayne** is a data-driven, grassroots open-source platform (AGPL-3.0) designed to educate citizens, policymakers, and researchers about the hidden economic and social costs of proposed casino expansions.

## Core Objectives
- **Public Education**: Expose documented social costs like addiction, crime, and wealth extraction.
- **Data Transparency**: Provide interactive models to visualize community deficits, ensuring all data and logic are open-source and reproducible.
- **Policy Critique**: Refute developer marketing claims with independent academic studies (e.g., Grinols, Welte) and empirical data.
- **Advocacy**: Mobilize the community to demand public referendums on casino projects.

The platform distinguishes itself by using deterministic, rule-based mathematical formulas derived from peer-reviewed economic literature rather than opaque "black box" consulting studies.
